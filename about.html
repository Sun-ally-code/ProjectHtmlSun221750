About Coffee Heaven

More than just a coffee shop — a community space in the heart of Lavington.

When you walk through our doors, the first thing you'll notice is the smell of freshly roasted beans and warm cinnamon. The second thing? The quiet hum of conversation, the clinking of cups, and maybe someone typing away on their laptop in the corner.

We designed this space to be comfortable, not pretentious. Wooden tables. Soft lighting. A mix of armchairs and bar stools. Somewhere you can stay for twenty minutes or three hours without feeling rushed.

Our walls are covered with local art that changes every few months. The music is always something mellow - think jazz, soul, acoustic covers. Nothing too loud. Nothing distracting.

Come for the coffee. Stay for the atmosphere.

From Bean to Cup

We source our beans directly from small-scale farmers in Kiambu and Nyeri. Every batch is roasted in-house, which means you're always getting coffee that was roasted within the last two weeks. No stale beans. No shortcuts.

Our espresso machine is a La Marzocca - the same brand used in some of the best coffee shops worldwide. It's an investment we made because we believe the equipment matters as much as the beans.

A Space That Welcomes You

The shop has about 40 seats spread across different zones. Need to focus? Grab a table near the window. Want to chat with friends? The back area has larger tables and softer seating. Working on your laptop? Every table has a power outlet nearby.

We also have a small outdoor patio for when Nairobi decides to give us good weather. It's shaded, quiet, and popular during late afternoons.

What Makes Coffee Heaven Different
