Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before visiting Coffee Heaven

Location Hours Delivery

We are located in Lavington, Nairobi. Our exact address is along James Gichuru Road, next to the Lavington Mall.

We are open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On weekends (Saturday and Sunday), we open at 8:00 AM and close at 6:00 PM.

Yes! We offer oat milk, almond milk, and soy milk as alternatives. We also have vegan pastries available daily — just ask our staff.

Absolutely! We have high-speed free Wi-Fi for all customers. The password is displayed at the counter. Every table also has a power outlet for charging your devices.

Yes! We recommend booking in advance for groups larger than 4 people. You can book a table online here or call us directly.

Yes! We deliver through Bolt Food and Uber Eats. You can also call us directly for large orders. Delivery is available within a 5km radius.

We accept cash, M-Pesa, and all major debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard). We also accept Apple Pay and Google Pay for contactless payments.

Yes! We have free customer parking available right outside the shop. There's also additional street parking along James Gichuru Road if our lot is full.

Absolutely! We allow outside cakes for birthdays and celebrations. Just let us know when you book your table so we can prepare plates and cutlery for you. A small serving fee of KES 200 applies.

Yes! We're a family-friendly space. We have high chairs available for toddlers, a children's menu (smaller portions), and colouring sheets to keep little ones entertained.

Yes! We sell our freshly roasted coffee beans in 250g and 500g bags. You can choose between our signature blend or single-origin beans from Kiambu and Nyeri. Just ask our baristas at the counter.

Yes! Our shop is available for private events such as birthday parties, baby showers, and team meetings. We can accommodate up to 40 people. Please contact us for availability and pricing.

Yes! We're a pet-friendly cafe. Well-behaved dogs are welcome on our outdoor patio. We even have a fresh water bowl available for your furry friends.

Yes! Our loyalty program gives you a free drink after every 10 purchases. Just ask for a loyalty card at the counter. We also send exclusive offers to our newsletter subscribers.
